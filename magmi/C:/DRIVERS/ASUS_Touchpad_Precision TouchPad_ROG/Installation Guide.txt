ASUS Precision TouchPad Driver Installation Guide 
ASUS Precision 觸控板驅動程式安裝說明

1.  Download and decompress Driver to desktop  
1.  下載並解壓縮檔案至桌面


2.  Right click mouse on Install_PTP.bat to install touchpad driver using administrator privileges.
2.  在Install_PTP.bat檔案上滑鼠點擊右鍵, 並使用管理者權限安裝

3. After installation, you may confirm completion in Device manager. 
3. 安裝完成後可至裝置管理員中確認


注意事項:  在安裝觸控板驅動程式前請先安裝Intel Serial IO驅動程式

Note: please install Intel Serial driver before installing Touchpad driver
